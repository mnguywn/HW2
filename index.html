Welcome to the May Institute

The May Institute is a nonprofit organization serving individuals with autism spectrum disorder and other developmental disabilities, brain injury and neurobehavioral disorders, and other disabilities. We are a well-known organization that has provided a range of rehabilitative services and exceptional education for thousands of families and children for the past 65 years.

Our corporate headquarters is located in Randolph, MA. However, we have successfully expanded our organization throughout the state of Massachusetts and the United States. From Georgia to California, we are determined to offer our support and services through applied behaviorial analysis.
